Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving growth through creativity and technology, this pillar focuses on research, startups, and modern tech like AI and IoT. STEM education, entrepreneurship, and public-private partnerships will enable a knowledge-based economy, making Bangladesh globally competitive.

Empowerment

Empowering citizens through education, skills development, and equal opportunities is vital. Focus on technology access and leadership programs will enable all to contribute to national progress.

Sustainability

Prioritizing renewable energy, green technologies, and eco-friendly practices, sustainability ensures balanced growth. Initiatives like sustainable urban planning and climate-resilient agriculture aim to preserve resources for future generations.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure will support economic growth through improved transportation, digital connectivity, and urban development. Smart cities, efficient public services, and better logistics will bridge gaps between rural and urban areas.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Promoting renewable energy sources to ensure sustainable development and reduce environmental impact.

Modern Infrastructure

Building smart, efficient systems to support economic growth and improve quality of life nationwide.
